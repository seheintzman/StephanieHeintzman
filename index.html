process flow

  • Audit
  • Strategic  Planning
  • Execution
  • Evaluation

An audit is a thorough examination of all factors relevant to your brand so you know where your business stands. Here is data collection and analysis is key.

Factors that should be evaluated include current business goals, challenges, ROI of current marketing efforts, industry trends, the competition and public perspective.

Once factors that influence the current state of your business are surfaced, and insights are known, it is time to focus on the opportunity

that’s best for long/short term success. With a creative eye and data driven insights, the ‘big idea’ is defined with compelling rational.

After developing a strategy, it's time to determine what tools to use to tell your story. S.M.A.R.T (specific, measurable, attainable, relevant and timely) goals are developed for each tactic based anticipated results/ ROI.

There are an infinite number of options to choose from (e.g. social media, events, programmatic media). Remember these tools should offer utility, and build a cohesive, memorable, 1-to-1 experience.

Once all deliverables have been executed, it is important to keep track, find out what tools are working most effectively, and adjust plans as needed.

By evaluating financial routine, success rates according to set goals, you will know you are on the right path to success.
